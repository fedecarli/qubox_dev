About Us

QuBox® is an engineering company that acts predominantly in scientific research and development of systems aimed to the financial market, more precisely in developing financial solutions, analytical, with hardware acceleration, low latency and high yield for high frequency negotiations, based on FPGA hardware.

We believe that technology must always work towards solving a problem or proposing a better solution to an already solved problem. For this reason, our team is always committed to understanding the trends of the world and to structuring a line of thought through reflections on themes related to the events that announce these trends. This helps us build a solid vision that guides us as to how we should position ourselves to succeed within the context of the world.

Following this guideline and our natural instinct to seek innovation by expanding our areas of expertise and action, we have decided that it is time to take a step towards the development of products related to the semiconductor industry. It was seeking to position us as suppliers of solutions that provide simplicity and security, guaranteeing in a mature way the sustainability and consolidation of the company.

Our culture of learning, continuous innovation and philosophy of rewarding excellent performance based on merit attract brilliant people from all over the world. We believe in the synergy that exists in combining the talent and passion of the new generations with the knowledge of professionals with great experience acquired over many years in the development market. We value our people and pride ourselves on being a diverse set of individuals. Our staff has a distinguished background in technology companies, world-renowned research labs, leading universities and PhD programs.

So we invest in this integration, promoting innovation and always stimulating our team in constant growth through the search for winning new challenges. Our ambition is to act as protagonists through the provision of disruptive technologies that contribute to this transformation that is occurring in the world economic scenario.
